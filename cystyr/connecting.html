Beja, Portugal [2019]

250 cientists independently work to solve a puzzle built to be solved through cooperation.
publish, or perish?
a mesh of 25 controllers operates 100 panels. only the correct orientation of all of them solves the puzzle.
in 30 minutes a micro-cosmos of society emerged, with annointed leaders, resistance, and a solved puzzle.

with Isaque Sanches and Goncalo Guiomar using bonsai-rx, unity3D, Ableton Live.
